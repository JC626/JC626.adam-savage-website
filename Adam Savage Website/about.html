About Adam Savage

Personal Life

Adam Whitney Savage was born in July 15,1967 in New York. His father was a filmmaker, animator and painter. His mother is a psychotherapist. He has one sibling, a sister who is an artist. He currently resides in San Francisco, California with his wife Julia Ward and twin sons.

Adam is an avid costume maker and cosplayer and has cosplayed as Jack Sparrow, Indiana Jones and Han Solo. His skills involve model making and building many things out of various materials. He is also proficient at juggling.

Work

Adam is currently a co-host in the prominent Discovery Channel TV show Mythbusters with Jamie Hyneman and has been part of the show since it's inception in 2003. Adam gained popularity through the science entertainment show where they test and bust myths through scientific experiment. He is also part of the website Tested.com and co-host of the weekly podcast Still Untitled: The Adam Savage Project

Other past professions include:

  1. Special Effects artist in movies including The Matrix Reloaded
  2. Teacher in Advanced Model Making in Industrial Design at the Academy of Art University in San Francisco
  3. Co-host of the TV Show Unchained Reactions
